# OTOBI AI vs Heyflow — Comparison and What to Pull

**Reference:** [Heyflow](https://heyflow.com/) — No-code funnel, form, and landing page builder for lead generation.  
**Date:** March 2026  
**Sources:** Heyflow homepage, pricing, product updates, blog (site crawl + fetched pages).

---

## 1. What Heyflow Is

Heyflow is a **lead-generation-focused** platform built around:

- **No-code builder** for **interactive funnels, forms, and landing pages** (mobile-first).
- **Pay for leads, not traffic** — response-based pricing (€49–€1,100+/mo by plan; add-ons per response).
- **Single platform** to replace: funnel builder, landing page builder, form builder, Zapier, Optimizely/VWO, Mailchimp, Twilio (they claim “from €79/mo” vs “$400+/mo” traditional stack).
- **50+ direct integrations** (CRM, ads, analytics, Calendly, WhatsApp, etc.).
- **Trusted by 3,000+ lead gen marketers** (AXA, BCG, Forbes, Rocket Mortgage, etc.); 24% conversion lift, 3M+ leads, 92% satisfaction, 200+ 5-star reviews (G2 4.4, OMR 4.5, Capterra 4.7).
- **Compliance:** ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2; EU hosting.

**Positioning:** “Paid ads bring clicks. We turn them into leads.” Typeform/Jotform/Outgrow alternative for **B2C/B2B lead gen, recruiting**, and industries (solar, real estate, insurance, financial, legal, home improvement). Strong for **performance marketers, agencies, enterprise**.

## 2. Heyflow Feature Map (from site)

### Build & Design
- **40+ drag-and-drop blocks:** Google Maps, Calendly, video, loader, etc.; mobile-ready.
- **Design 100% on brand:** custom fonts (Google or upload), full CSS, custom domain, remove Heyflow logo (white-label).
- **Embed** on any site or custom domain.
- **Custom code:** JavaScript, HTML, custom head (Coding bundle).
- **Built-in:** payments (Stripe, Solidgate), signatures, bookings (Calendly).

### Engage & Qualify
- **Conditional logic & advanced navigation** — personalize by choices/data; quizzes, branching.
- **Variables** — personalized experience.
- **Scores and custom results** — lead scoring, dynamic pricing (formulas/calculations).
- **Custom follow-up emails** (SMTP, custom domain).
- **Lead quality:** phone validation, **SMS OTP**, address (Google Maps).

### Connect & Integrate
- **50+ integrations:** Matomo, Google Sheets, Airtable, Slack, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pipedrive, Calendly, YouTube, WhatsApp, Webflow, Webhooks, Stripe, TrustedForm, Openli, Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Analytics, GTM, Zapier, Recruitee, Hotjar, LeadByte, Personio, Jornaya, Zoho, Monday, ActiveCampaign, Voluum, HighLevel, Pabbly, etc.
- **Webhooks** (advanced: custom headers, HTTP methods, extended payload).
- **Meta Conversions API, TikTok Conversions API** — native server-side conversion tracking.
- **Agency portal** — white-label tracking for agencies and clients.

### Analyze & Optimize
- **Native A/B testing** inside the builder (split traffic to funnel/form variations; statistical confidence).
- **Drop-off analytics** — where users leave.
- **Page speed scores > 90** (claimed).
- **Lead scores** (calculated).
- **Hotjar, Microsoft Clarity** — heatmaps, session recordings, behavior.

### Scale & Deploy
- **Agency:** collaborator accounts (€35/account/mo), agency portal.
- **Enterprise:** custom security, import funnel services, advanced support, unlimited funnels, 5,000+ responses/mo.
- **Easy deployment** — embed snippet, custom domain.

### Security & Compliance
- **TrustedForm** — TCPA proof-of-consent.
- **Sensitive data tag**, IP capture.
- **GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO 27001.**

### Pricing model
- **Starter** €49 — 5 funnels, 50 responses/mo, core bundle.
- **Growth** €89 — 10 funnels, 250 responses, Design + Conversion bundles (phone validation, SMS OTP, payments, custom fonts, remove branding).
- **Scale** €239 — 20 funnels, 1,000 responses, A/B testing, webhooks, WhatsApp, custom JS/HTML, Insights/Coding/Advanced integrations/Security bundles.
- **Enterprise** from €1,100 — custom, unlimited funnels, 5,000+ responses.
- **Bundles:** Design €40, Conversion €40, Insights €40, Coding €90, Advanced integrations €40, Security €30, Success €100; **response add-ons** from €0.15/response.

## 3. OTOBI AI vs Heyflow — Side-by-Side

| Area | Heyflow | OTOBI AI |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Funnel builder** | No-code, 40+ blocks, mobile-first, interactive (quiz/branching) | Multi-step funnels (landing, form, payment, thank-you); steps linked to landing pages/forms; no visual block library yet |
| **Form builder** | Part of funnel + standalone; rich blocks | Standalone forms (text, email, phone, textarea, select, checkbox, number); share link; responses → leads |
| **Landing pages** | Same builder as funnels; 40+ blocks | Template-based builder; components; AI generate; no block library (Maps, Calendly, video, etc.) |
| **Lead qualification** | In-flow: logic, variables, scores, OTP, phone validation | Lead scoring in CRM; assignment; round-robin; no in-form OTP or phone validation |
| **Payments** | Stripe, Solidgate in-flow | Stripe for our subscriptions; funnel step type “payment” (Stripe price ID); no in-form Stripe block |
| **Bookings** | Calendly block | Not built-in |
| **Signatures** | Signature block | Not built-in |
| **Google Maps / address** | Block + address suggestions | Not built-in |
| **A/B testing** | Native in builder (split traffic to variations) | A/B testing for campaigns/creatives; not inside funnel/form builder |
| **Drop-off analytics** | Per-step drop-off | Analytics/events; no funnel-step drop-off view |
| **Page speed** | Claimed >90 | Not exposed |
| **Integrations** | 50+ native (CRM, ads, Calendly, Hotjar, etc.) | Webhooks/Zapier; ad platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok); no Calendly/Hotjar/Clarity/TrustedForm native |
| **Meta / TikTok Conversions API** | Native server-side | Not native (we have ad platform connections; no CAPI) |
| **Webhooks** | Advanced (headers, methods, payload) | Webhook endpoints for events |
| **Agency / white-label** | Agency portal; remove branding; collaborator accounts | Team + roles; no agency portal or “white-label client” view |
| **Custom code** | JS, HTML, custom head (Scale) | Not in funnel/landing builder |
| **Custom domain** | Yes | Landing pages have published URL; custom domain not productized |
| **Compliance** | TrustedForm, sensitive tag, IP, GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 | GDPR-aware; no TrustedForm or HIPAA/SOC 2 positioning |
| **Pricing model** | Response-based (pay per lead); bundles | Subscription tiers (not per-lead) |
| **Content & AI** | Minimal (positioning = funnels/forms/leads) | **22 content types, repurposing, brand voice, multi-language, ads, intelligence** — much broader |
| **Ads & campaigns** | Track conversions; no ad creation | Full campaign creation, ad performance, creative, scheduling |
| **Reviews / reputation** | No | Yes (reviews dashboard) |
| **One-click reports** | No | Yes (shareable report links) |
| **AI agents / chat** | No | Yes |

**Summary:** Heyflow is best-in-class for **no-code, mobile-first lead-gen funnels and forms** with rich blocks, lead quality (OTP, validation), native integrations, and response-based pricing. OTOBI AI is stronger on **content, AI, campaigns, intelligence, and breadth** (reviews, reports, lead distribution, etc.) but has a **simpler funnel/form/landing experience** and fewer “conversion blocks” and native integrations.

## 4. What We Should Pull From Heyflow (Prioritized)

### High impact, fits our stack

1. **Funnel drop-off analytics**  
   - **What:** Per-step conversion/drop-off (e.g. step 1 → 2 → 3 and drop-off % at each step).  
   - **Where:** Funnel detail page or Analytics.  
   - **Why:** Directly improves funnel optimization; we have funnels and analytics but not step-level drop-off.

2. **A/B testing inside funnel/form**  
   - **What:** Split traffic between 2+ funnel (or form) variations; declare winner by conversion/statistical significance.  
   - **Where:** Funnels (e.g. “Duplicate as variation” / “Run A/B test”) and optionally Forms.  
   - **Why:** They push this hard; we have campaign A/B tests but not in-funnel A/B tests.

3. **Lead-quality blocks (conceptual)**  
   - **What:** Phone validation (format/region), optional SMS OTP (e.g. Twilio), and/or address/Google Maps in forms/funnels.  
   - **Where:** Form builder (new field types or “blocks”), funnel steps that use forms.  
   - **Why:** Reduces junk leads; aligns with “qualify and convert” positioning.

4. **Block library for landing/funnel**  
   - **What:** Reusable blocks: Video embed, Google Maps, Calendly (embed or link), Signature, maybe Calculator/Formula.  
   - **Where:** Landing page builder and/or funnel step config (when step = landing or form).  
   - **Why:** Their “40+ blocks” is a key differentiator; we have components but not this set.

5. **Page speed / performance hint**  
   - **What:** Simple page speed score (e.g. Lighthouse or similar) for published landing/funnel URLs, shown in UI.  
   - **Where:** Landing page or funnel detail.  
   - **Why:** They claim “page speed > 90”; we can add a basic “health” signal.

### Medium impact (nice to have)

6. **Custom code (JS/HTML) in funnel/landing**  
   - **What:** Optional custom script or HTML block/snippet for a funnel or landing page.  
   - **Where:** Funnel step or landing page “Custom code” section.  
   - **Why:** Power users and agencies; they charge for it (Coding bundle).

7. **White-label / remove branding**  
   - **What:** Option to hide “Powered by OTOBI AI” (or similar) on shared forms/landings/funnel pages.  
   - **Where:** Settings or per-funnel/per-form/per-landing.  
   - **Why:** They offer “remove Heyflow logo”; we can match for agencies.

8. **Industry/use-case templates**  
   - **What:** Funnel/form/landing templates by use case (e.g. Real estate, Solar, Insurance, B2B lead gen) in addition to generic ones.  
   - **Where:** Template picker in Funnels, Forms, or Landing pages.  
   - **Why:** They segment by industry and role; we can improve discovery and time-to-value.

### Lower priority or different model

9. **Response-based pricing**  
   - **What:** Optional plan or add-on: pay per lead/response (e.g. €X per response above quota).  
   - **Why:** Their core model; we are subscription-based. Only consider if we explicitly target “pay per lead” positioning.

10. **Native Meta/TikTok Conversions API**  
    - **What:** Server-side conversion send to Meta/TikTok from our backend when a lead is created (e.g. from form/funnel).  
    - **Why:** Strong for ad optimization; requires API setup and compliance. Can come after we have stable funnel/form conversion events.

11. **Agency portal**  
    - **What:** Dedicated view for agencies: client list, per-client funnels/leads/reports (white-label).  
    - **Why:** We have Team, not client sub-accounts; bigger product shift. Defer unless we commit to agency-as-customer.

12. **Hotjar / Clarity integration**  
    - **What:** Embed or link to Hotjar/Clarity for heatmaps/session replay on funnel/landing URLs.  
    - **Why:** They integrate natively; we could add “analytics snippet” or integration later.

## 5. One-Line Summary

**Heyflow:** Best-in-class **no-code lead-gen funnel/form/landing** builder with 40+ blocks, lead quality (OTP, validation), 50+ integrations, native A/B testing and drop-off analytics, response-based pricing.  
**OTOBI AI:** Broader **AI marketing platform** (content, campaigns, intelligence, reviews, reports, lead distribution); funnel/form/landing exist but are simpler and with fewer blocks and fewer “conversion” features.  
**What to pull:** Funnel **drop-off analytics**, **in-funnel/in-form A/B testing**, **lead-quality options** (phone validation, optional OTP, address/Maps), a **block library** (video, Maps, Calendly, signature, formula), and a **page speed** signal. Optionally: custom code, white-label, and industry templates.

## 6. Suggested Next Steps

1. **Implement funnel drop-off analytics** — persist step views/submissions and expose “funnel health” (conversion rate and drop-off per step) in UI.  
2. **Add A/B test for funnels** — e.g. “Create variation” of a funnel and split traffic (50/50 or configurable); show winning variant by conversion.  
3. **Extend form/funnel blocks** — add block types: Video embed, Google Maps (and/or address), Calendly link/embed, Signature; later consider SMS OTP (Twilio) and phone validation.  
4. **Add page speed check** — run Lighthouse (or similar) on published landing/funnel URL and show score in dashboard.  
5. **Document and roadmap** — add Heyflow to `COMPETITIVE_RANKING.md` in the funnel/forms category and reference this doc for the “what to pull” list.
